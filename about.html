About Our Orphanage

OrphanLife Foundation is a non-profit orphanage dedicated to providing care, protection, and opportunities to orphaned and vulnerable children. Our mission is to ensure that every child grows up with dignity, hope, and access to a better future.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a safe and nurturing environment for orphaned children by supporting their education, health, emotional well-being, and daily needs. We believe every child deserves love, stability, and the chance to succeed.

Our Vision

We envision a world where no child is left without care, where orphaned children are empowered to become confident, educated, and independent adults who can positively impact their communities.

Our History

OrphanLife Foundation was established with a simple but powerful idea: to give orphaned children a place they can truly call home. What began as a small initiative to support a few children has grown into a trusted organization serving many vulnerable children over the years.

In its early days, the orphanage focused on providing basic necessities such as food, clothing, and shelter. As the needs of the children evolved, so did our work. We expanded our programs to include education support, healthcare services, emotional counseling, and life-skills development.

Through the dedication of our founders, staff, volunteers, and supporters, OrphanLife Foundation continues to grow and adapt, ensuring that every child under our care receives not just support, but a real opportunity for a brighter future.
